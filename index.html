What we collect

Money Buddy does not collect your name, email address, location, or any personally identifying information.

When you use Group Sync, Google Firebase generates an anonymous authentication token (UID) stored on your device. This UID has no connection to your real identity.

Trip data you choose to share via Group Sync is uploaded to Google Firebase Firestore. This includes trip names, expenses, contributor names, and journal entries. Receipt photos are never uploaded — they remain device-local only.

When you make an in-app purchase (Remove Ads), RevenueCat processes the transaction. RevenueCat stores an anonymous customer ID and your purchase receipt to validate entitlements. No personally identifying information is shared with RevenueCat beyond what is required to process and verify your purchase.

Advertising (free version)

The free version of Money Buddy displays ads served by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect and use data to serve relevant ads, including:

• Device identifiers (advertising ID)
• IP address (used to approximate general location for ad targeting)
• App interaction data (e.g. which ads were viewed or tapped)

This data is collected and processed by Google LLC under their Privacy Policy. Money Buddy does not have access to this data.

You can remove all ads permanently by purchasing "Remove Ads" ($2.99 one-time) inside the app under Settings. Once purchased, no ad SDKs will serve content to you.

You can also opt out of personalised ads at any time through your device settings:
• Android: Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalisation
• iOS: Settings → Privacy → Tracking → disable for Money Buddy

Third-party services

Google Firebase — Group Sync uses Firebase Authentication and Firestore. These services are operated by Google LLC and governed by the Google Privacy Policy. Firebase Authentication is used in anonymous mode only — no personal identity is associated with the token.

Google AdMob — Serves ads in the free version of the app. Governed by the Google Privacy Policy. See the Advertising section above for details.

RevenueCat — Processes and validates in-app purchases. Governed by the RevenueCat Privacy Policy. Only anonymous purchase data is shared — no personal information.

ExchangeRate-API — Live exchange rates are fetched from open.er-api.com. Only currency codes are sent in these requests — no personal data whatsoever.

Data retention & deletion

Local data (trips, settings, receipts) is stored in your device's app storage and is deleted when you uninstall the app or tap Settings → Data & Reset → Reset All App Data.

Cloud data (Group Sync trips) persists in Firebase until you explicitly delete it. Uninstalling Money Buddy does not automatically delete cloud-synced trip data.

To remove cloud data: open the shared trip → Trip Settings → Danger Zone → Delete Trip.

Purchase data is retained by RevenueCat to validate your entitlement. To request deletion of RevenueCat data, contact us at the email below.

Your rights
